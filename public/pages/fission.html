Fission App
Concept
 
An e-commerce platform which allows users to put clothes on custom avatars as a virtual fitting room, aiming to revolutionize the online shopping experience.
Challenge

How might we increase product reliability and ensure a realistic representation of products online to reduce return rates amongst customers?
My Role

Ideation, Visual Design, UX Design, User Research, Competitive Analysis
Date

2022
Timeline

3 months
Tools

Figma, Procreate, Photoshop
01 Analyze
Context
This design is part of an e-commerce business project.
Despite the continually growing e-commerce market, customers are unable to interact with products they see online, leading to high expectations for products which are rarely met when the products arrive at the doorstep.
2021 data from Shopify shows that just over 70% of customer returns are due to shopper preference in which the wrong sizing of products makes up 52% of returns.

Problem definition
The problem can be neatly summed up as follows:
How might we increase product reliability and ensure a realistic representation of products online to reduce return rates amongst customers?

The ideas
Brands have already begun to incorporate 3D renders of their clothing into their websites as virtual runways or interactive models.
Fission can use a similar technology which considers fabric, material, and construction to put clothes onto a custom avatar (created by the user) to see how the clothing item fits on different body types.
  • Customizable avatars: Users input their measurements, or upload their pictures to Fission, and Fission will create a custom avatar for them.
  • Creating fits: Users can pair multiple clothes together to see how they match, whether from their own closet or a new item.
  • Social aspect: Users can share the fits they create for their 3D models and view other peopleâ€™s fits for inspiration. Influencers can also recreate the fits they wear on their social media on Fission to share with their followers.

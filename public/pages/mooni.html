mooni.ai
Prototype
 
A high-fidelity prototype with interactive components and animated elements for an AI based tax software.


Challenge

How might we simplify the tax filing experience and inspire mooni.ai users to consistently use the platform?
My Role

UX Design, Interaction Design, Visual Design, Brand Design, Prototyping, Usability Testing
Date

February 2023
Timeline

2 weeks
Tools

Figma, Midjourney
01 Understand
Context
mooni.ai is a startup which uses artificial intelligence to search through the extensive IRS tax code to find every single tax refund and credit that's available to the user. Like a personal AI accountant, mooni.ai makes tax filing and finding tax benefits easy for the user.
Goal
Our team's main goal was to figure out how to include all the elements which the founders wanted to incorporate into the app while making sure the visuals are clean and friendly.
02 Explore
Ideate + Iterate
We worked with a couple of different visual designs for this app.

We were aiming for clean grayscale visuals for the first iteration of the app. I created a framework for the onboarding and a wireframe of the home screen, but I quickly found that it lacked visual interest and uniqueness due to the color limitations.

Our clients chose to scrap the black and white theme in favor of a more colorful gradient based theme on a dark background. After creating an iteration with the dark theme, we decided to experiment with a light theme, which we ultimately converged on for the final prototype.
User Flows + Interaction Models
There were a couple of main interactive elements which we hoped to incorporate into the design.
  • A responsive wheel to show viewers how much money they can save with mooni.
  • An animated orb-like AI which users can interact with in a series of questions and answers.
  • Selection carousels for onboarding.
